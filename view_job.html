Job Description

As a Senior Frontend Developer, you will be responsible for the development of the frontend of our web applications. You will work closely with the product team to develop new features and improve the user experience of our applications. You will also be responsible for mentoring junior developers and helping them grow their skills. You will have the opportunity to work on a wide range of projects and technologies, and you will have the opportunity to make a real impact on the success of our products.

    Responsibilities

  • Develop new features for our web applications
  • Improve the user experience of our applications
  • Mentor junior developers
  • Work closely with the product team to define requirements
  • Work with the backend team to integrate frontend and backend code
  • Write clean, maintainable code
  • Participate in code reviews
  • Contribute to the technical design of our applications
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest frontend technologies
